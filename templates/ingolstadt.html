About

It's been two years since I moved to Germany to finish my master's degree and recently I decided I want a place where I can share my hobbies with others. Because I like journaling but also coding, I saw this as a opportunity to get the best of both sides and spend some time doing what I love!

With parks, museums and castles, Ingolstadt, also know to be the place where Victor Frankenstein, one of the most well know monsters in the literature was created, is a typical bavarian city.

Nestled along the banks of the Danube River in the Bavarian region of Germany, the historic city of Ingolstadt beckons visitors with its rich heritage, charming streets, and vibrant culture. As one of Bavaria's oldest cities, its roots trace back to the Roman Empire, but it gained prominence during the Middle Ages as a thriving trade center and later as the birthplace of the famous car manufacturer, Audi.

The imposing fortress of Ingolstadt, known as the Bavarian Landesfestung, stands as a testament to the city's strategic importance throughout history. Built in the 15th century, it once served as a formidable defense against invaders and today offers panoramic views of the city from its ramparts.
